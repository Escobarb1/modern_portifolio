Most Basic Information About Dogs

A dog is a domestic mammal of the family candae and the order carnivora.Its scientific
name is Canis Lupus familiaris.Dogs are a subspecies of the gray wolf,and they are also
related to foxes and jacals.Dogs are one of the two most ubiquitous and most popular
domestic animals in the world.
